Our Mission

E-Sumbong is committed to empowering individuals, communities, and organizations worldwide to become catalysts for environmental change. Through our innovative mobile application, we enable users to report environmental issues directly to relevant agencies. We aggregate this data to provide actionable insights for policymakers and stakeholders, fostering a proactive approach to environmental conservation.

Our Vision

Our vision is to transform E-Sumbong into a global platform where concern for the environment translates into collective action. By focusing on Sustainable Development Goals 9, 13, 14, and 15, we aim to create a vibrant community dedicated to preserving and enhancing our planet. We envision a future where every individual can contribute to the sustainable development of our world, ensuring a healthier planet for future generations.
