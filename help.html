Frequently Asked Questions

E-Sumbong is a platform dedicated to empowering individuals, communities, and organizations worldwide to drive environmental change. We provide an innovative mobile application that allows users to report environmental issues directly to relevant agencies, creating a channel for effective communication and action.

E-Sumbong aligns with Sustainable Development Goals 9, 13, 14, and 15, focusing on industry innovation, climate action, life below water, and life on land. By addressing these key areas, we strive to create a vibrant global community dedicated to preserving and enhancing our planet for current and future generations.

E-Sumbong reports environmental issues to relevant Philippine government agencies such as the Department of Health (DOH), Department of Social Welfare and Development (DSWD), Department of Public Works and Highways (DPWH), and others based on the nature of the reported issue. We ensure that concerns are directed to the appropriate authorities for swift and effective resolution.

You can easily report environmental issues using our mobile application. Simply download the E-Sumbong app from your app store, create an account, and follow the intuitive reporting process. Your report will be forwarded to the relevant agencies for prompt action.
